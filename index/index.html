Call for Papers

近年來，台灣音樂劇的創作動能可說有目共睹，製作數目連年成長，作品更展現多元的題材、風格與形式...

Over recent years, the production of musical theatre in Taiwan has grown substantially. Theatre companies are presenting musicals in a wide variety of styles...
